Pointcloud & 3D Constructions

This video presents a pointcloud composition of various plant structures. The pointclouds were created through photogrammetry and assembled in Blender into a single spatial composition. The animation was produced in TouchDesigner, using real-time point rendering and motion controls.
This video presents a pointcloud scan of a cactus structure, where each point is represented by an ASCII character. The animation reacts in real-time to audio from Sonic Pi and explores the relationship between organic form and symbolic behavior.
Pointclouds from different architectural monuments, combined at varying scales. The work explores visual overlap between historical sites.
