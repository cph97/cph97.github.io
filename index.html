Botanic Pointcloud Composition

This video presents a pointcloud composition of various plant structures. The pointclouds were created through photogrammetry and assembled in Blender into a single spatial composition. The animation was produced in TouchDesigner, using real-time point rendering and motion controls.

Cactus Scan Visualization

This video presents a pointcloud scan of a cactus structure, where each point is represented by an ASCII character instead of a standard particle. The animation was created in TouchDesigner and reacts in real-time to a custom audio signal generated with Sonic Pi.

Latent Viral Form – GAN-Derived Protein Morphology with Wave Deformation (SARS-CoV-2)

This video presents a synthetic viral structure generated from a custom-trained PointNetGAN model on SARS-CoV-2 spike protein datasets. The latent output, reconstructed as a .pdb-like geometry, was visualized and animated in Blender using wave-based deformations. The result is a pulsating digital morphology — a speculative viral form shaped by data synthesis and artistic manipulation. This project explores how neural networks reinterpret biological patterns, and how movement can reintroduce symbolic agency to data-born forms.

Latent Viral Form – GAN-Derived Protein Morphology (HIV Dataset)

This image captures a synthetic structure generated through PointNetGAN, trained on point cloud data extracted from viral protein models (PDB). The form shown here deviates from recognizable protein topologies, embodying a latent-space abstraction shaped by learned viral geometries. The output does not replicate biological accuracy, but instead reflects how neural networks interpret structural probability in high-dimensional space. It exists between simulation and speculation — a hallucinatory virological form shaped by data, but free from biochemical constraint.

Procedural Weather Simulation – Dynamic Landscape Study

This video presents a dynamic landscape and procedural weather simulation built in Unreal Engine 5. The terrain was generated in Gaia using EEG (electroencephalography) data collected from the artist, transforming a moment of mental activity into topographic form. The environment evolves in real time through randomized weather transitions — including sun, rain, storms, and snow — reflecting both internal and external fluctuations. The work explores bio-data-driven landscape design and procedural systems for audiovisual environments.

Latent Viral Form – GAN-Derived Protein Morphology (Hepatitis C Virus Dataset)

This video presents a speculative protein structure generated from a custom-trained PointNetGAN model using data derived from Hepatitis C virus (HCV) proteins. The GAN was trained on 3D point cloud representations of viral protein geometries, extracted from Protein Data Bank (PDB) entries related to HCV. The shown structure reflects a latent-space interpretation of HCV morphology — not as an accurate replication, but as a generative abstraction of viral spatial properties. The output was reconstructed into a .pdb-like geometry and visualized in Blender. Through this hybrid approach, the work explores synthetic biology as form, asking how data-driven systems reshape our perception of molecular identity and pathogenic aesthetics.
