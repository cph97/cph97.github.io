3D Artist | Audiovisual Experiments & Spatial Compositions

Botanic Pointcloud Composition

This video presents a pointcloud composition of various plant structures. The pointclouds were created through photogrammetry and assembled in Blender into a single spatial composition. The animation was produced in TouchDesigner, using real-time point rendering and motion controls.

Cactus Scan Visualization

This video presents a pointcloud scan of a cactus structure, where each point is represented by an ASCII character instead of a standard particle. The animation was created in TouchDesigner and reacts in real-time to a custom audio signal generated with Sonic Pi.

Fragment Composition – Gothic Architecture Space

This video shows a single-shot walkthrough of a game environment created in Unreal Engine 5. The space is populated with stylized humanoid figures and dynamically triggered lighting.

Multi-Site Pointcloud Composition – Monument Overlap Study

This video presents a pointcloud composition based on photogrammetry scans from different architectural monuments, including arches and fountains. The pointclouds were combined in Blender at varying scales and spatial alignments.

Multi-Site Pointcloud – Audio-Driven Transformation

This video presents a variation of the multi-site composition, where architectural scans respond dynamically to audio signals. Animated in TouchDesigner through scale, position, and opacity modulation.

Plasmodium Protein 6E10 – Visualization & Sonification

This video presents a mirrored visualization of the Plasmodium protein structure 6E10. The atomic geometry was mapped to sound parameters in Pure Data for real-time sonification.

SARS-CoV-2 Spike Protein – Simulation & Sonification

This video shows a custom OpenMM simulation of SARS-CoV-2's spike protein. Atomic coordinates were mapped to sound frequencies in Pure Data, forming a coordinate-driven audiovisual transformation.

Pointcloud Composition – Interior Space

This video presents a pointcloud of an interior space, procedurally deformed in TouchDesigner with noise-based displacement to introduce spatial fragmentation.

Reflected Plant Structure

This pointcloud scan of a plant was mirrored in Blender and made audioreactive in TouchDesigner, driven by custom audio generated in Pure Data.
